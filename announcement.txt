Subject: New Astro-Coffee paper browser for Princeton Astrophysical Sciences

Hi everyone,

I'm excited to share a new web tool for our daily Astro-Coffee meetings:

  https://changgoo.github.io/astro-coffee-page/

The page gives you a clean, fast view of the day's arXiv astro-ph listings,
with a few features tailored to our department.

Author matching is done by comparing the name as it appears on the arXiv
paper against a list of ~170 department members. The matching rules are:

  • Strong match (bold amber): last name matches exactly AND first name
    matches exactly, OR last name + first initial + agreeing middle initial.

  • Weak match (italic grey): last name + first initial only.

Common reasons your name might not be highlighted (or highlighted
incorrectly):

  • You publish under a different form of your name than what appears on the
    department people page (e.g., middle name as first name, hyphenated names,
    diacritics handled differently).

  • You are not yet on the people page (new arrivals, some visitors).

If your name isn't appearing as expected, please let me know — you can
reach me by email (changgoo@princeton.edu), Slack, or by opening a GitHub
issue at https://github.com/changgoo/astro-coffee-page/issues/new.
I can add you manually to the author list right away.

Any other feedback — unclear UI, missing features, bugs — is very welcome
through the same channels.

Hope this makes coffee prep a little easier!

Best,
